What is a Pillow Doll?
It is a collectible artsy rarity, made of cloth and stuffed with fluff, which can be used as (or confused for) a pillow or a doll, given the preponderance of illustrated creatures with two (2) or more eyes. Its most notable functions include being a supportive traveling companion, a surprisingly silent coworker, a quirky decorative element and a comfy snuggler.
